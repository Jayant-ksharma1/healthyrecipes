PASTA

Pasta is a classic Italian dish made from durum wheat flour, water, or eggs. It's boiled to perfection and served with various sauces, such as marinara, pesto, or Alfredo, depending on preference.

This quick and versatile dish is enjoyed worldwide. You can choose from different pasta shapes and sizes, such as penne, fusilli, or spaghetti, to create countless variations of pasta dishes, each one more delicious than the last.

Whether served with a tangy tomato sauce, a rich creamy sauce, or a light olive oil-based dressing, pasta is always a crowd-pleaser, making it perfect for both casual dinners and special occasions.



HOW TO PREPARE IT

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil or butter and saut√© garlic, onions, and your choice of vegetables or meat.
3. Add your preferred sauce (tomato, cream-based, etc.) and simmer for a few minutes.
4. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce until fully coated.
5. Garnish with fresh herbs, grated cheese, and serve hot.
