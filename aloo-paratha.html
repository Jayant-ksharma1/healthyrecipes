ALOO PARATHA

Aloo Paratha is a popular North Indian breakfast dish made with whole wheat flatbread stuffed with a flavorful spiced mashed potato filling.

This dish is best enjoyed with a dollop of butter, yogurt, or pickle. Aloo Paratha is often served as a complete meal, especially during chilly mornings when the warmth and spices make it truly comforting.

It’s a beloved dish for breakfast or lunch and is frequently seen on tables during festivals and family gatherings, where it’s enjoyed by everyone from young children to adults.



HOW TO PREPARE IT

1. Boil potatoes and mash them with spices such as cumin, coriander powder, garam masala, and green chilies.
2. Make dough with whole wheat flour, water, and salt.
3. Roll out small portions of dough and fill them with the prepared spiced potato mixture.
4. Roll out the stuffed dough into flatbreads and cook them on a griddle (tava) with ghee or oil until golden brown and crispy.
5. Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or butter for a delicious breakfast or meal.
