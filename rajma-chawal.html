RAJMA CHAWAL

Rajma Chawal is a beloved North Indian dish that brings together kidney beans (rajma) cooked in a spicy, flavorful gravy served alongside fluffy basmati rice (chawal). This dish is often enjoyed as a hearty lunch or dinner, and it's comfort food at its best.

The combination of tender kidney beans in a rich tomato-based gravy is complemented perfectly by the mild, aromatic rice. This dish is also a source of plant-based protein and is often served with a side of yogurt or pickle to balance the flavors.

Rajma Chawal is especially popular in homes across Northern India, where it’s a staple for family meals. It is often prepared in large quantities and shared among friends and loved ones, making it a symbol of togetherness and comfort.



HOW TO PREPARE IT

1. Start by soaking the kidney beans (rajma) overnight or for 6-8 hours.
2. Pressure cook the soaked rajma with water until soft.
3. In a pan, heat oil and sauté onions, ginger, and garlic.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft, then add spices like cumin, coriander powder, garam masala, and red chili powder.
5. Add the cooked rajma to the pan and cook for 10-15 minutes to let the flavors blend together.
6. Serve the rajma with steamed rice (chawal) and enjoy your delicious Rajma Chawal.
