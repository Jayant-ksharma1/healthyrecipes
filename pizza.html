Pizza



Pizza is one of the most beloved and versatile dishes from Italy. It's made with a delicious dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. Here's a simple recipe to make your own homemade pizza from scratch!

HOW TO PREPARE IT



Making pizza involves several steps, from preparing the dough to baking the pizza. Below are the details:

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast (about 2 ¼ tsp)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Various toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, bell peppers)

Instructions:

  1. Activate the yeast by mixing it with warm water and sugar. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir to form a dough.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions.
  6. Roll each portion into a pizza base on a lightly floured surface.
  7. Transfer the rolled dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the dough.
  8. Top with mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot!

Tips:

  • If you want a thinner crust, roll the dough thinner before baking.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as cheddar, Parmesan, or goat cheese.
  • You can also add a drizzle of olive oil or sprinkle some dried herbs (e.g., oregano, basil) on top for extra flavor.
