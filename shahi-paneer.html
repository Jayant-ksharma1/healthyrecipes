SHAHI PANEER

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy dish from Mughlai cuisine, featuring cubes of soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a luxurious gravy made with cashews, cream, and aromatic spices.

This dish is a perfect treat for special occasions and celebrations. The mildly sweet and spicy gravy complements the paneer cubes, creating a balance of rich flavors. Itâ€™s typically served with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

Shahi Paneer is not only a favorite in Indian households but is also enjoyed by food lovers worldwide for its indulgent taste and regal origins.



HOW TO PREPARE IT

1. Start by making a paste of soaked cashews, onions, and tomatoes.
2. In a pan, heat ghee or oil, add the prepared paste, and cook until the mixture turns golden brown.
3. Add cream, garam masala, and cardamom powder to the gravy.
4. Gently cook the paneer cubes in the gravy until tender and well coated.
5. Garnish with saffron strands or fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
